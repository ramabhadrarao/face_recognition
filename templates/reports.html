Policy Recommendations

  • NO_PAY: Strictest policy, prevents time theft but may be harsh for genuine issues
  • HALF_DAY: Balanced approach, gives some credit for attendance
  • FULL_DAY: Most generous, good for trusted employees
  • ACTUAL_HOURS: Fairest for part-time or flexible workers
